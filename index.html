tatia burdett :: software developer

I'm a software developer primarily located in Denver, CO, however I travel often, and can be found anywhere from Seattle to Europe. I've spent most of my professional career working as a nurse in urgent cares, hospitals, and long term care facilities.

I am passionate about mental and physical health, sustainability, and personal growth. I'm interested in helping to create a more accessible world, to inspire healthy choices, and to create lasting change in our society. Technically speaking, I am experienced using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

About

I've worked as a nurse for over 8 years, however I was never passionate about nursing. I fantasized often about spending my days working on my laptop staring at a page full of code. I often critisized the charting software I used in the workplace, and imagined up new, functional platforms that would make nursing life easier. Finally, I made the choice to start learning how to code - first on my own, then I joined Bloc to gain more structure.

If you are looking for a professional, hard-working software developer, please reach out and contact me. I am also happy to connect with other developers and designers to collaborate on new projects!

When I'm not coding, I keep myself occupied with a variety of stimulating hobbies, including music production, photography, yoga, and travel / exploration.
